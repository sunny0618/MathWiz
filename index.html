You are the only one left in your kingdom. All the brave warriors and wizards have perished in a counter attack against the invaders. Now it is up to you, a math wizard, to defeat the monsters with your math abilities.

Use your speed and problem solving abilities to defeat the monsters.

1) To eliminate a monster, solve the math equation over top the wizard's head

2) Click on the corresponding monster with the correct answer

3) Eliminate all the monsters before they reach the castle!
